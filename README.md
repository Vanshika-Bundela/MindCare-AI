# MindCare AI

## AI Powered Mental Wellness & Stress Prediction Platform

MindCare AI is a modern AI-powered mental wellness platform designed to help users monitor, analyze, and improve their mental health using Machine Learning and Full Stack Web Development.

The platform predicts a user’s mental state based on lifestyle habits such as:
- screen time
- social media usage
- sleep patterns
- physical activity
- social interactions

It also provides:
- personalized wellness suggestions
- emotional tracking
- focus & relaxation tools
- PDF wellness reports

---
# Problem Statement

In today’s digital world, excessive screen time, unhealthy social media usage, poor sleep habits, and reduced physical activity are significantly impacting mental health, especially among students and young adults.

Most individuals fail to recognize early signs of stress and do not have access to continuous emotional wellness monitoring.

MindCare AI aims to solve this problem by providing an intelligent and accessible AI-based mental wellness ecosystem.

---

# Proposed Solution

MindCare AI combines:
- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Data Visualization
- Full Stack Development
- Emotional Wellness Tracking

to create a platform where users can:
- assess stress levels
- monitor wellness trends
- receive personalized suggestions
- improve emotional wellbeing
